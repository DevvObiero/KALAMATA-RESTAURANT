From Sunrise to Starlight

Nestled high above the city on our cozy indoor floor, Botanica invites you to indulge in an extraordinary dining experience. From the first light of breakfast to the twilight of dinner, our curated menu showcases the best in farm-to-table dishes.

Step onto our lush green terrace and escape the urban buzz as you dine amidst the foliage, enjoying the panoramic views of Nairobi. This oasis in the sky is the perfect backdrop for a leisurely lunch or an intimate dinner under the stars.

THE BAR

As the sun sets, elevate your experience at the trendiest Gin Bar in town, exclusively at Botanica. Located on the 7th Floor at One Africa Place, our Gin Bar is a destination for connoisseurs and trendsetters alike, offering an array of innovative blends and classic concoctions. Join us at Botanica for an unforgettable journey through the senses, where every dish tells a story and every moment is a celebration. Whether you’re a breakfast enthusiast, a lunchtime explorer, or a dinner connoisseur, our doors are open to welcome you.
